About WBS Online

Winfield Business School Online brings Africa's most relevant leadership, management, and startup programs to one modern learning platform.

Our mission

Accelerate ambitious professionals and founders through pragmatic programs that blend Winfield Strategy expertise with the agility of Hatch Business Academy.

  • Executive learning for growth-focused leaders.
  • Practical startup tracks curated with Zimbabwean innovators.
  • Work-ready certificates trusted by regional employers.

Impact snapshot

  • 18K+ learners enrolled across Africa.
  • 1,200+ enterprise teams enabled by WBS for Business.
  • 45 industry mentors and faculty supporting every cohort.

What makes us different

WBS Online combines executive polish with startup speed. Every program includes applied projects, community feedback, and direct access to facilitators who have shipped products, raised capital, or led cross-border teams.

Leadership

Our team brings together strategists from Winfield Strategy, faculty from Hatch Business Academy, and guest experts from healthcare, finance, logistics, and the public sector. Together we maintain rigorous quality standards and keep our curriculum aligned to Zimbabwean and regional priorities.
