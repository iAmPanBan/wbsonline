Privacy policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Data we collect

We collect profile information, course activity, assessments, payment history, and communication preferences when you use WBS Online. We may also receive limited data from partners who sponsor your learning.

2. How we use data

  • Deliver and personalize Winfield and Hatch programs.
  • Provide certificates, transcripts, and progress analytics.
  • Improve curriculum through aggregated and anonymized insights.

3. Sharing

We share data with payment processors, cloud hosting providers, and enterprise customers who enroll their teams. Every vendor signs data protection agreements aligned to POPIA and GDPR standards.

4. Your choices

Email privacy@wbsonline.africa to request data exports, corrections, or deletion.
