About Us

Winfield Strategy & Innovation is a Pan-African consulting firm helping organizations build winning strategies and drive sustainable growth through innovation. We specialize in strategy development, design thinking, corporate governance, and organizational transformation.

Our team partners with businesses, governments, and non-profits to unlock potential, accelerate market penetration, and build resilient, future-ready institutions. From board evaluations and executive training to innovation systems and governance frameworks, we deliver practical, high-impact solutions.

We proudly serve clients across Zimbabwe, South Africa, Zambia, and Botswana, empowering them to lead confidently in dynamic and evolving markets.
